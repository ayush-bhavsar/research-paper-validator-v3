Research Paper Validator

Demonstrate blockchain integrity for academic work. Upload a PDF, preview it, generate a hash, and sign it via MetaMask.

How It Works

  1. Connect Wallet: Click "Connect MetaMask" to link your Ethereum wallet
  2. Upload PDF: Drag & drop or select your research paper (max 10 MB)
  3. Preview: Review your document with page navigation controls
  4. Generate Hash: Click "Validate Paper" to create a SHA-256 cryptographic hash
  5. Sign: Approve the signature request in MetaMask (no gas fees)
  6. Verify: Receive signature proof with hash, timestamp, and signer address
