How the Validator Works

Follow these simple steps to verify the authenticity of a research paper using decentralized technology.

Step 1

Upload & Inspect

Select a PDF file from your device. The app renders an instant preview so you can double-check that you picked the correct document.

Step 2

Generate Unique Hash

We compute a SHA-256 fingerprint directly in your browser. No file content ever leaves your machine, keeping confidential work private.

Step 3

Commit to Blockchain

With MetaMask, sign a transaction that stores the hash on the Ethereum blockchain (Sepolia testnet by default). The ledger makes tampering virtually impossible.

Step 4

Share the Proof

Use the transaction hash and timestamp to prove the document existed in its current form at the recorded moment. Anyone can independently verify it.

Why Blockchain?

  • Immutable, time-stamped evidence of publication.
  • Decentralized verification without relying on a single authority.
  • Transparent audit trail for peer reviewers and institutions.

Best Practices

  • Validate new revisions to maintain a verifiable history.
  • Share only the hash and transaction details, not confidential content.
  • Use test networks while experimenting, then move to mainnet for production proofs.
